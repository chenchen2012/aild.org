## What changed this week

- Agentic AI governance playbooks emerged, providing structured frameworks for managing autonomous AI systems.
- McKinsey released a new AI leadership playbook advocating for flatter teams and faster execution cycles.
- AGN IT Services introduced a structured AI framework specifically designed to drive SME adoption.
- BetaNXT announced plans to move wealth management AI from pilot to production phase.
- Shared memory technology was highlighted as a key enabler for smarter AI collaboration across systems.

## Leadership implications

- CIOs must integrate governance into enterprise AI initiatives to ensure responsible scaling.
- HR leaders are taking center stage as companies push to scale AI across the enterprise, focusing on workforce strategy.
- Analytics and AI are reshaping IT leadership boundaries, requiring new operating models.
- The enterprise AI readiness gap persists, with data infrastructure being the real barrier to scale.
- Tech workforce strategies directly impact digital readiness and AI implementation success.

## Decisions for the next 7 days

- Review current AI governance frameworks against new agentic AI playbooks.
- Assess organizational structure for potential flattening to accelerate AI execution.
- Evaluate SME adoption barriers and consider structured frameworks like AGN's approach.
- Plan transition strategies for moving AI projects from pilot to production phases.
- Initiate discussions on shared memory systems to enable better AI collaboration.
