## What changed this week

- HHS aligned health technology leadership to deliver an AI-enabled healthcare system, emphasizing data liquidity and affordability.
- Analytics and AI are reshaping IT leadership boundaries, requiring new operating models.
- Three out of four global leaders prioritize AI investment despite economic uncertainty, according to KPMG's survey.

## Leadership implications

- Agentic AI governance frameworks are emerging to make AI enterprise-ready, requiring structured oversight.
- HR leaders are taking center stage as companies push to scale AI across the enterprise, highlighting workforce strategy importance.
- An AI trust gap exists where performance requires control, emphasizing the need for robust governance in decision-making.

## Decisions for the next 7 days

- Assess enterprise AI readiness gaps using company data to identify real barriers to scale.
- Review and align AI governance frameworks with agentic AI playbooks to ensure operational confidence.
- Prioritize investments in AI workforce strategies to impact digital readiness and support scaling initiatives.
