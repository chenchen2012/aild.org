## What changed this week

- New expectations for enterprise AI leadership emphasize decision-making and governance frameworks.
- Research shows plug-and-play AI is a myth, with enterprise leaders increasingly rejecting it for custom builders.
- AI governance best practices were showcased, highlighting proprietary insights for enterprise resilience.
- AI is changing the role of CISOs, requiring new approaches to security and trust in the age of agents.

## Leadership implications

- Leaders must embrace 'thinking mode' with AI to enhance decision-making and strategic agility.
- Optimizing IT infrastructure for AI is critical to support custom solutions and workforce agility.
- Analytics leadership mastermind groups can address the 'last mile' of AI implementation and governance.
- Building trust in AI systems requires updated governance models and resilience strategies.

## Decisions for the next 7 days

- Review current AI governance frameworks against showcased best practices and update as needed.
- Assess IT infrastructure readiness for custom AI solutions and plan optimizations.
- Initiate discussions on forming or joining analytics leadership mastermind groups to tackle AI implementation challenges.
- Evaluate security and trust protocols for AI agents, incorporating insights from CISO role changes.
